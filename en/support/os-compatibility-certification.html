OS Compatibility and Certification Matrix

System Motherboard OS Distribution Support Level OS Install on Storage Level
Support Level:
OS Installed - The operating system has been tested on the hardware by Supermicro for basic compatibility
OS Certified - The operating system has been tested and certified on the hardware by the OS vendor's hardware compatibility test suite
Validated - The operating system has been both 'Installed' and 'Certified' on the hardware per the above

LEGEND:
  • A2 – i-SATA/SATA (w/o RAID, AHCI mode)
  • A3 – i-SATA/SATA (w/ RAID)
  • A4 – sSATA (w/ RAID)
  • A5 – sSATA (w/o RAID, AHCI mode)
  • A6 – tSATA (w/o RAID, AHCI mode)
  • A6R – tSATA (w/ RAID)
  • B1 – SAS (IT mode)
  • B2 – SAS (IR mode)
  • B3 – SAS (SR/IMR mode)
  • C1 – NVMe
  • C2 – NVMe w/ RAID
  • D1 – UIO (LSI 2008 IR Mode)
  • D2 – UIO (LSI 2008 IT Mode)
  • D3 – UIO (LSI 2008 iMR Mode)
  • D4 – UIO (LSI 2108 MegaRAID 4.x)
  • D5 – UIO (LSI 2208 MegaRAID 5.x)
  • D6 – UIO (LSI 2308 IR Mode)
  • D7 – UIO (LSI 2308 IT Mode)
  • D8 – UIO (LSI 3008 IR Mode)
  • D9 – UIO (LSI 3008 IT Mode)
  • D10 – UIO (LSI 3108 MegaRAID 6.x)
  • E1 – AEP
