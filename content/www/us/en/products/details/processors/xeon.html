Choose Intel® Xeon® 6 for Ansys Fluent Workloads

Intel® Xeon® 6 processors enhance database and analytics performance by leveraging P-cores for complex relational workloads and E-cores for high-throughput, task-parallel non-relational workloads. Their high core counts, large memory capacity, and integrated accelerators like Intel® QAT and DSA enable efficient handling of numerous small, independent data requests, offloading encryption, compression, and data movement tasks to free up CPU resources.
